Activities And Company's details

The company has been set up as a Limited Liability Company (Sole Proprietorship) and it was officially launched 20 years ago, as a family business. The main activity of the company is distribution of quality wines produced in Macedonia (in particular the Kumanovo region) to wholesalers and retailers (restaurants, supermarkets, wine stores). The company has extensive experience in wine distribution on the domestic market, as well as on the regional market (Serbia and Croatia). In 2018 company the company was rebranded (KOVIN export-import DOOEL) A new business concept and strategy was developed and adopted including sales and promotional strategy. The changes were initiated and implemented by the new owner and manager (family business successor), Mrs Marija Stojanovska.

“My goal is to ensure continuation of the family business by implementing new business concepts and strategies that will enable building of profitable, sustainable and socially responsible company which will be recognized on the domestic as well as the European market. Since I was a kid, I have been involved in the business activities of the company from different aspects, which gave me the opportunity to develop the necessary skills for wine sales and promotion. Currently, I am enrolled in a university, in a course for economics, finances and accounting. My ambition is to build a career through self-employment and entrepreneurial initiatives and as a business woman to help other women who seek employment or want to start their own business. I strongly believe in the concept of lifelong learning and I will strive to create employment opportunities and offer trainings for personal development for the company’s employees and women with less opportunities and single mothers from the North-eastern region of the country. The social aspect is very important part of the company’s vision and hopefully it will become more and more visible as the company develops. The field of distribution management is a large and exciting field with great career opportunities, salaries and earnings. Distribution managers or employess, generally work standard hours from Monday to Friday in office, which is very convenient for women that would like to balance family and career”

Marijana Stojanovska manager of the wine distribution company Kovin, Macedonia

Our mission

identifying and offering a selection of high quality wines that provide the best value for customers at all price points. We are committed to deliver exceptional services to all of our clients and to build trustworthy and sustainable partnerships.

Our vision

KOWINE is committed to sustainable development and strives to become trusted and remarkable wine broker and distribution company in the wine industry of the Macedonian and European market, that continuously aims to add value to its customers, employees and owners.
